QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything operators ask before hiring a legal staff from Zedtreeo.

How much does it cost to hire remote legal staff?+

Zedtreeo remote legal staff start from $8/hour ($1,280/month, $15,360/year). Entry-level paralegals and doc review specialists sit at the base rate; senior paralegals and contract reviewers with niche expertise (M&A, IP, privacy) sit higher. Total cost is 70–90% below onshore.

Which legal roles can you staff?+

Paralegals, legal assistants, contract reviewers, contract drafters, e-discovery specialists, doc review attorneys (non-US-bar), legal researchers, compliance analysts, IP/trademark filing specialists, litigation support, and legal billing admins.

How do you handle attorney-client privilege and confidentiality?+

Mutual NDA and confidentiality agreements at contract, ISO 27001-certified infrastructure, VPN-only access to case files, role-based permissions in your DMS, audit logs on every file access, no external drives, and privilege-aware workflows. We train staff specifically on US/UK/AU attorney-client privilege rules.

Can your paralegals work on US litigation matters?+

Yes — for support work. Our paralegals assist with case preparation, doc review, deposition summaries, e-discovery, and filing support. They don't practice law or sign filings — that stays with your licensed attorneys. This is the standard model for LPO (legal process outsourcing).

How fast can we onboard a legal support hire?+

Shortlist in 48 hours. Skills test and interview within 3–5 days (we test on actual contract review, not theoretical questions). Onboarded in your DMS/case management tool within 7–10 business days. For urgent doc review matters we can stand up a 5-person team in 10 days.

Do you support specific practice areas or software?+

Yes. We match on practice area (contracts, litigation, IP, privacy, corporate, real estate) and on software (Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther, NetDocuments, iManage, Relativity, Logikcull). If you're a Relativity shop, your shortlist is pre-filtered for Relativity-certified staff.

What about ethics rules around outsourcing legal work?+

US, UK, and AU ethics rules generally permit outsourcing legal support work provided the attorney supervises, client informed consent is obtained where required (varies by jurisdiction), fees are not marked up to the client, and confidentiality is maintained. We operate within these frameworks. Consult your jurisdiction's rules for specifics.

Can we scale the team for a big matter and then scale down?+

Yes. E-discovery and doc review are the most common flex-capacity use cases. We can pre-stage 10–20 trained reviewers within 5–7 business days, run them for the duration of the matter, and demobilize when the project ends. You pay only for active capacity.
