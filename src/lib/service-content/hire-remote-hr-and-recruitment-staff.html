QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything operators ask before hiring a hr and recruitment staff from Zedtreeo.

How much does it cost to hire a remote recruiter from Zedtreeo?+

Remote HR and recruitment staff at Zedtreeo start from $6/hour ($960/month, $11,520/year). Exact rate depends on specialization — a sourcer sits at the entry tier, while a senior full-cycle technical recruiter or HRBP-track ops lead sits higher. Total cost lands 70–90% below onshore and a fraction of agency contingent fees.

Do your recruiters do sourcing only or full-cycle?+

Both. We staff specialized sourcers for agencies and in-house teams that need top-of-funnel volume, and we staff full-cycle recruiters who own sourcing, screening, scheduling, candidate experience and offer admin end-to-end. For HR ops, we also place coordinators for onboarding, offboarding and HRIS admin.

What ATS and HRIS platforms do they work in?+

Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workday, iCIMS, Workable on the ATS side. BambooHR, Rippling, Workday, Gusto, Justworks on HRIS. Sourcing tools include LinkedIn Recruiter, Gem, Hired, hireEZ, SeekOut. We onboard into your tools — no platform lock-in, no rebuild required.

How fast can we onboard a recruiter?+

Shortlist of 2–3 pre-vetted recruiters within 48 hours. Interview loops including a live sourcing exercise run same-week, and the hired recruiter starts Monday of the following week. End-to-end from JD to first qualified candidate submitted is 7–10 business days for most role types.

Is this better than using agency recruiters on contingency?+

For volume hiring, almost always yes. Agency contingency fees run 15–25% of first-year salary — $15K–$35K per placement. A Zedtreeo recruiter at $11,520/year can deliver 20–40 hires annually. The math changes dramatically past 4–5 open roles. Agencies still make sense for rare executive searches.

How do you handle candidate confidentiality and data?+

Mutual NDA at contract, ISO 27001 infrastructure, VPN-only ATS access, role-based pipeline permissions, GDPR-compliant data handling, and disabled external drives. Zedtreeo recruiters sign the same confidentiality agreements as your in-house team — candidate PII is handled with full compliance.

Can they run candidate interviews for us?+

Yes — for initial phone screens, calibration interviews and recruiter screens. Some clients have Zedtreeo recruiters run final-round interview coordination and candidate experience loops as well. Hiring-manager and technical interviews stay with your team, which is the right split for bar calibration.

What happens if a recruiter underperforms?+

We run a 5-day free trial at the start. If fit isn't right, no bill. Post-trial, we measure against agreed metrics — pipeline throughput, submission-to-interview rate, time-to-fill — and replace within 7–14 days at no additional cost if targets slip. Our retention sits above agency averages because recruiters stay 2–3+ years.
