What BSS/OSS and utility systems do your staff work in?+

Salesforce Communications Cloud, ServiceNow TSM, Netcracker, Amdocs, Oracle BRM, SAP ISU, SmartGridCIS, Open Smartflex, ESRI ArcGIS, Smallworld, GE Digital Grid, Itron Enterprise Edition, Siemens EnergyIP, SolarWinds, LogicMonitor, Grafana. Match on exact stack during vetting.

Can your team run 24/7 NOC monitoring with actual triage (not just escalation)?+

Yes. Tier 1 pods handle monitoring, initial triage, ticket creation, customer notification and documented escalation. Tier 2 pods handle deeper investigation, config changes and known-good-runbooks. Your tier 3 stays onshore; offshore handles everything below that 24/7.

Do your compliance staff understand FCC, OFCOM, ACMA, CRTC, FERC, NERC and PUC filings?+

Yes. Compliance staff are trained on FCC (US telecom), OFCOM (UK), ACMA (AU), CRTC (CA), TRA (UAE), FERC, NERC CIP, state PUC filings, ROE, tariff schedules and standard telecom/utility regulatory cycles. They prepare; your licensed regulatory affairs team signs.

How fast can we onboard telecom and utilities staff?+

Shortlist of 2–3 pre-vetted candidates within 48 hours. BSS/OSS access, runbook walkthrough and sandbox tickets in week one. Most CSRs and NOC tier-1 are on live coverage under supervision by day 5–7.

How do you handle NERC CIP and other security-sensitive environments?+

All staff operate under NDA, VDI-only access with documented CIP controls, recorded training, quarterly access reviews and SOC 2 Type II-aligned operations. For NERC CIP and FERC environments, we run a separately controlled enclave with US-persons-only access where required.

Can we hire one CSR or do we need a full pod?+

Either. Small WISPs and MVNOs often start with a 2-person CS + dispatch pod. Larger telcos and utilities run 20–50 person pods across NOC, customer ops, dispatch, billing and compliance. We'll recommend based on subscriber count and service territory.

What if a NOC tier-1 misses a critical incident?+

5-day free trial. Post-trial every NOC hire has defined KPIs — time-to-acknowledge, triage accuracy, escalation quality, runbook adherence. Replacement within 7–14 days at no cost if quality drops. Most NOC pods beat onshore benchmarks on acknowledgement-time within 60 days.
