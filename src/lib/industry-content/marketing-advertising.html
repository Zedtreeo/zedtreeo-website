Yes. We staff overlapping shifts for clients across the US, UK, Europe, Middle East, Canada, Australia and New Zealand. Yes. Scale up with new hires in 48 hours for launches, Q4 pushes or seasonal campaigns; scale down with 30 days' notice. No termination penalties, no severance. Most brands start with 2–3 roles and expand to 6–10 within the first year as marketing output compounds.
