What IT roles can you staff?+

IT support (Tier 1/2/3), sysadmins, junior and mid-level full-stack developers, DevOps and SRE, cloud engineers (AWS, Azure, GCP), cybersecurity analysts and SOC staff, QA automation engineers, DBAs, network engineers and technical project managers.

Can you provide 24×7 coverage?+

Yes. Our timezone spread enables follow-the-sun support and on-call rotations. For 24×7 SOC, NOC or IT support, we staff across multiple shifts with proper handoff documentation. You get continuous coverage without burning out your onshore team.

How fast can we onboard an IT hire?+

Shortlist of 2–3 pre-vetted candidates within 48 hours. Technical screen, live coding challenge or troubleshooting simulation, and stack-specific matching complete in week one. Most IT hires are in sandbox access and first tickets by day 7.

Can you staff cybersecurity and SOC roles?+

Yes. We place SOC analysts (L1/L2/L3), vulnerability management specialists, GRC analysts, cloud security engineers, pen testers and SIEM engineers. Certifications include CompTIA Security+, CEH, CISSP, Azure/AWS security specialties. ISO 27001-certified work environments.

Do you handle IP, code confidentiality and GitHub access?+

Yes. Mutual NDA, IP assignment, VPN-only GitHub/GitLab access, ISO 27001-certified offices, disabled USB/external drives, and full audit logs. Your source code stays in your repos — no offline copies, no personal-device access.

Can I hire one engineer or do I need a pod?+

Either. Many clients start with one senior engineer and scale to pods of 3–10 as they grow. MSPs often staff Tier 1/2 pods of 8–15 from day one for 24×7 client coverage. We recommend the shape that fits your roadmap — not a fixed package.

What if an engineer isn't the right fit?+

We run a 5-day free trial at the start. If fit isn't right, no bill. Post-trial, we track output (tickets closed, PRs merged, deploy success rate, code review quality) and replace within 7–14 days if performance drops. Retention sits well above the industry 14-month US average.
