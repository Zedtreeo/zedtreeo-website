Frequently Asked Questions

Everything operators ask before hiring a SaaS and technology company staff from Zedtreeo.

How much does remote SaaS and technology staffing cost at Zedtreeo?+

SaaS and tech staff starts from $8/hour ($1,280/month, $15,360/year). Support reps, SDRs, onboarding specialists and RevOps admins sit at the entry tier; senior CSMs, implementation consultants and DevOps support run $9–14/hour. Total cost lands 70–90% below onshore equivalents.

What SaaS stack do your staff work in?+

HubSpot, Salesforce, Outreach, Apollo, Salesloft, Intercom, Zendesk, Freshdesk, Gainsight, ChurnZero, Catalyst, Vitally, Totango, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Segment, Hotjar, Looker, Metabase, dbt, Snowflake, Stripe, Chargebee, Notion, Jira, Linear, GitHub. Match on exact stack during vetting.

Can your CSMs handle named enterprise accounts?+

Yes for mid-market and commercial. For enterprise/strategic accounts, we typically run a hybrid: onshore named CSM, offshore associate CSM handling health monitoring, QBR prep, renewal ops and expansion ideation. You get strategic coverage without strategic headcount cost.

Can your support team cover 24/7 ticket SLAs?+

Yes. Global timezone coverage is standard — ideal for global SaaS products where customers expect 24/7 response. Shift-based handovers, documented escalation paths and tight product expertise across shifts, not shared BPO agents.

Do your SDRs actually dial, or just send email?+

Both. Our SDRs run full multi-touch sequences — email, LinkedIn, phone — with calling accents trained for US, UK, AU and CA prospects. We run A/B testing on sequences and report booked-meeting rate, not activity vanity metrics.

How do you handle data security and SOC 2?+

All staff operate under NDA, VDI-only access to your production systems, documented access reviews and SOC 2 Type II-aligned controls. GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA (if applicable) workflows documented. We don't pull customer data to local machines — period.

Can we hire one CSM or do we need a full pod?+

Either. Seed/Series A startups often start with one CS generalist + one support rep. Scaling SaaS build pods of 10–30 across CS, support, onboarding, SDRs and RevOps. We'll recommend based on ARR, account count and current team size.

What if a CSM doesn't hit retention targets?+

5-day free trial on live accounts. Post-trial, every CSM has defined KPIs — GRR on book, NRR on book, expansion created, QBR completion, CSAT. Replacement within 7–14 days at no cost if quality drops. Most CSM pods beat onshore benchmarks within 90 days.
