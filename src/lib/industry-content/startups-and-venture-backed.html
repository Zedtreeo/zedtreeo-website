Frequently Asked Questions

Everything operators ask before hiring a startup and venture-backed company staff from Zedtreeo.

How much does remote startup staffing cost at Zedtreeo?+

Startup staff starts from $6/hour ($960/month, $11,520/year). EAs, CX reps, SDRs and ops admins sit at the entry tier; senior ops generalists, marketing ops leads and founding associates run $7–10/hour. Total cost lands 70–90% below onshore equivalents — runway extender.

Can your staff work in our modern stack (Notion, Linear, Slack, HubSpot)?+

Yes. We vet on Notion, Linear, Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, Intercom, Stripe, Mercury, Brex, Ramp, Rippling, Gusto, Deel, Carta, DocSend, Pitch, Figma, Retool and the full modern-startup stack. Match on exact stack before placement.

Can one person do ops + EA + CX work like a founding team member?+

Yes — that's our sweet spot. We hire generalists vetted for range (ops, CX, EA, finance admin, vendor mgmt) and venture-pace judgment. Pre-seed and seed-stage founders often start with one full-stack operator who runs all admin for $960/month.

How does this work across timezones for real-time collaboration?+

Most startup staff overlap 3–5 hours with US/UK hours. For real-time async teams (Slack-first), our staff lean into full async ops — overnight you get a clean inbox, scheduled calls confirmed, vendor emails sent and board pack formatted.

Do your staff understand venture economics (burn, runway, ARR, CAC)?+

Yes. Startup pod leads are trained on burn math, runway calculations, ARR/MRR accounting, CAC/LTV, cohort analysis, investor update formats, cap table hygiene and board deck formatting. They speak founder-language, not corporate-admin-language.

How do you handle confidentiality for fundraising and sensitive data?+

NDA before placement. Cap table, fundraising decks, financial models and investor communications get VDI-only access, documented review logs and case-by-case clearance. Most founders add one dedicated ops person who handles this — no rotating staff on sensitive data.

Can we hire one operator or do we need a full pod?+

Either. Pre-seed and seed founders often start with one full-stack operator at 40 hours/week. Series A–C startups run pods of 3–10 across ops, CS, SDR, marketing ops, finance admin. We'll recommend based on stage, burn and bottleneck.

What if it's not working in the first month?+

5-day free trial first. Post-trial, no long contracts — month-to-month. Replacement within 7–14 days at no cost if fit isn't there. We know founders can't afford a bad hire — we optimize for fit in week one, not month three.
