Frequently Asked Questions

Everything operators ask before hiring a education and e-learning staff from Zedtreeo.

How much does remote education and e-learning staffing cost at Zedtreeo?+

Education staff starts from $5/hour ($800/month, $9,600/year). Enrollment VAs, LMS admins and student support reps sit at the entry tier, while senior instructional designers and Articulate developers run $6–8/hour. Total cost lands 70–90% below onshore equivalents once you factor in software, PTO and supervision overhead.

What LMS and authoring tools do your staff know?+

Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, D2L Brightspace, Docebo, TalentLMS, Cornerstone, Articulate Storyline 360, Articulate Rise, Adobe Captivate, Camtasia, Vyond, H5P and custom LMS platforms. We match on exact stack during vetting and confirm fluency before placement.

Can they design courses pedagogically — not just technically?+

Yes. Our instructional designers are trained in ADDIE, SAM, Bloom's taxonomy, Kirkpatrick evaluation, UDL (Universal Design for Learning) and WCAG/Section 508 accessibility. Many have masters in instructional design or formal education backgrounds. Content is pedagogically sound, not just technically produced.

Can your staff tutor students live over Zoom/Teams?+

Yes. Our online tutors run live 1:1 and small-group sessions over Zoom, Teams, BigBlueButton or proprietary platforms — K-12 supplemental, university-level math/science, language learning, test prep and corporate coaching. All tutors are native-quality English with subject-matter vetting.

How fast can we onboard education staff?+

Shortlist of 2–3 pre-vetted candidates within 48 hours. LMS access, SOP training and sandbox content in week one. Most enrollment VAs, LMS admins and student support reps are in live workflows by day 5–7; instructional designers typically ship first draft content by week 2.

Can they handle SCORM/xAPI packaging and QA?+

Yes. Our developers build, test and package SCORM 1.2, SCORM 2004, xAPI (Tin Can) and AICC content. They QA on multiple LMS platforms, debug completion/tracking issues and handle cross-browser testing. Many corporate L&D teams use us for production of CE/compliance libraries.

Can we hire one IT or do we need a full team?+

Either. Smaller platforms start with one LMS admin or instructional designer and scale to pods of 3–6 as courseware grows. Larger universities and EdTech platforms staff pods of 8–20 for content, LMS ops and 24/7 student support. We recommend structure based on catalog size and student volume.

What if an instructional designer's output doesn't meet our standards?+

We run a 5-day free trial at the start. If fit isn't right, no bill. Post-trial, we track output against agreed metrics (courses shipped, QA pass rate, student satisfaction) and replace within 7–14 days at no additional cost if quality drops. Retention sits above industry averages because we pre-match on pedagogy and tool fit.
